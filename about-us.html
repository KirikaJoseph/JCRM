Brief Overview

Jesus Come Revival Ministries (JCRM) is a vibrant community dedicated to spreading the transformative love of Jesus Christ. Rooted in faith and fueled by a passion for revival, we welcome all to join us on a journey of worship, discipleship, and service. Together, let's encounter Jesus and impact the world.

  • Our Mission

    At Jesus Come Revival Ministries (JCRM), our mission is to make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We're committed to spreading the gospel message, nurturing believers, and empowering individuals to live out their faith in love and service.,

  • Our Vision

    Our vision at Jesus Come Revival Ministries (JCRM) is to see lives transformed by the power of Jesus Christ. Inspired by the Great Commission, we envision a world where every soul encounters the love of God, experiences revival, and is equipped to fulfill their purpose in His kingdom.

  • Our values

    Our values at Jesus Come Revival Ministries (JCRM) are rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ. We value love, compassion, and inclusivity, embracing all who seek refuge and redemption. We uphold integrity, humility, and fervent prayer as we strive to serve others with grace and humility, reflecting God's love in all we do.

OUR HISTORY

Founded in the year 2000, JCRM (Jesus Christ Redeems Ministries) began as a small fellowship with a profound vision for community transformation. Over the decades, our commitment to spreading the love of Christ has flourished. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a vibrant congregation dedicated to serving our local community and beyond. Through outreach programs, spiritual guidance, and unwavering faith, we continue to impact lives, embodying the message of redemption and hope. JCRM's history is a testament to the power of faith and the transformative potential of God's love in action. Join us as we journey forward, guided by our rich heritage and vision for a brighter future.

Apostle Athur Lenax

Apostle Arthur Lenax Likhakasi, the esteemed founder of Jesus Christ Redeems Ministries (JCRM), is a beacon of faith, leadership, and divine inspiration. Born into humble beginnings, Apostle Likhakasi's journey has been marked by unwavering dedication to spreading the gospel and uplifting communities. His profound spiritual calling led him to establish JCRM in 1978, driven by a vision of transforming lives through the love and grace of Jesus Christ. Apostle Likhakasi's ministry is characterized by compassion, integrity, and a deep commitment to serving others. His dynamic preaching, rooted in biblical truth and personal revelation, has touched countless hearts and souls, leading many to embrace faith and find redemption in Christ. Read more
