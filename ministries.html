Christian Living

As part of what christ requires us to do, we ought to delve into the transformative power of salvation offered through Jesus Christ. Embracing love, compassion, and forgiveness, as we strive to mirror His teachings in our daily lives. Through prayer, study of Scripture, and fellowship, we ought to seek spiritual growth and deeper intimacy with God. As believers, we need to acknowledge Jesus as our Lord and Savior, embracing the truth that salvation comes through faith in Him alone. Our Christian journey is anchored in the assurance of salvation, motivating us to live lives that honor God and reflect His grace and mercy to others.

Outreach

In Outreach, we heed God's call to extend His love and compassion beyond the walls of our church. As the body of Christ, we're tasked with sharing the gospel and meeting the practical needs of others. Through outreach, we aim to fulfill the Great Commission, demonstrating God's love in tangible ways and inviting others into relationship with Him. JCRM has been a beacon of hope in our society, organizing food drives, shelters, and evangelistic efforts to positively impact our community.

Read More

Adults

In the body of Christ, adults are called to live lives of purpose and significance, guided by biblical principles. Ephesians 4:1 urges adults to live a life worthy of their calling, demonstrating humility, gentleness, patience, and love. Productivity in the body of Christ involves active participation in ministry, discipleship, and service. JCRM fosters this call by providing opportunities for adults to engage in small groups, mentoring relationships, and leadership development programs. Through intentional discipleship and modeling godly character, adults nurture the next generation of believers.

Read More

Youths

Youths are vital members of the body of Christ, called to actively participate in the life and ministry of the church. 1 Timothy 4:12 encourages them to set an example in speech, conduct, love, faith, and purity. Proper upbringing and mentorship equip youths to become strong and resilient leaders in the church and society. JCRM invests in youths through dedicated youth groups, mentorship programs, and opportunities for leadership development, empowering them to fulfill their God-given potential.

Read More

Children

Children are precious gifts from God, and the Bible emphasizes the importance of raising them in the knowledge and wisdom of God. Proverbs 22:6 instructs us to train up children in the way they should go, so that when they are older, they will not depart from it. It's the responsibility of the church and its members to provide a nurturing environment where children can grow spiritually and develop strong foundations of faith. JCRM actively engages in children's ministry through Sunday school classes, vacation Bible school programs, and family-oriented events, aiming to equip children to become resilient and exemplary members of society.
