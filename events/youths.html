Let Your Light Shine Youth Convention

Description:The Let Your Light Shine Youth Conference is a dynamic gathering designed to empower and equip young hearts and minds to shine brightly for Christ. From April 17th to 20th, join us for a transformative experience of worship, empowerment, and fellowship. Discover your identity, purpose, and potential as you dive into sessions tailored specifically for young believers. Be inspired by passionate speakers, engage in impactful outreach missions, and experience the power of worship and fellowship in our special services. Come ready to be equipped, challenged, and empowered to let your light shine brightly in every area of your life.

Topics Covered:

  • Discovering Your Identity in Christ
  • Unleashing Your God-Given Purpose
  • Navigating Leadership and Influence
  • Cultivating a Vibrant Spiritual Life
  • Engaging in Compassionate Outreach
  • Building Meaningful Relationships and Community

Question and answer sessions will be available so as to tackle any matter or issues affecting the youths

Eligibility:

The Let Your Light Shine Youth Conference is open to all youths aged 13 to 25, regardless of background or denomination. Come as you are and join us for an inspiring journey of growth, empowerment, and fellowship.

Skills you will gain:

  • Identity in Christ: Discovering who they are in Christ and understanding their value and purpose.
  • Leadership Skills: Learning how to lead with integrity and influence positively in their spheres of influence.
  • Spiritual Growth: Cultivating a deeper relationship with God through prayer, worship, and study of His Word.
  • Compassionate Service: Understanding the importance of serving others with love and compassion.
  • Building Community: Developing meaningful relationships with peers and mentors in a supportive community.
  • Embracing Purpose: Embracing their unique gifts and talents to make a difference in the world for God's kingdom.

Prerequisite:

None. All youths aged 13 to 25 are welcome to participate, regardless of their level of spiritual experience or background. Come with an open heart and a desire to grow in your faith, and join us for an inspiring and transformative experience.

Fee:

  • Registration Fee: 500 shillings per attendee.
  • This nominal fee covers conference materials, access to all sessions and workshops, and participation in outreach activities.
  • Payment can be made online during registration or in person at the conference venue.
  • Please note that financial assistance may be available for those facing hardship. Contact us for more information.

Duration:

  • The Conference will take place over four days, from April 17th to April 20th. from 10am to 5pm
  • Each day will feature a combination of sessions, workshops, worship gatherings, and outreach activities.
  • Participants are encouraged to attend all four days to fully engage with the conference experience and maximize their learning and growth opportunities.

Certificate Provided:

No
