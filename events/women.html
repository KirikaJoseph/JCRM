Women of Substance Conference

Description:Welcome to Women of Substance conference, where women are empowered to embrace their unique identity, cultivate their strengths, and make a meaningful impact in their communities. Join us for a series of transformative events designed to inspire, equip, and connect women from all walks of life. From uplifting conferences to intimate gatherings and special services, there's something here for every woman seeking to grow spiritually, personally, and professionally. Come, be part of a sisterhood of women who are making a difference and leaving a legacy of substance in their spheres of influence.

Event Offerings

  • Empowerment Conferences
  • Leadership Workshops
  • Women's Retreats
  • Networking Events
  • Special Services
  • Personal Development Seminars

Eligibility:

All women, regardless of age, background, or experience, are invited to participate in Women of Substance Events. Whether you're a seasoned leader or just starting your journey of personal growth, there's a place for you in our community of empowered women.

Lessons you will Learn

  • Skills You Will Gain: Leadership and empowerment skills.
  • Personal growth and development.
  • Networking and relationship-building.
  • Spiritual enrichment and growth.
  • Practical tools for success and fulfillment.
  • Community engagement and impact.

Prerequisite:

None. All women are welcome to attend with an open heart.

Fee:

  • Registration fees vary depending on the event and may include early bird discounts, group rates, and scholarship opportunities.
  • Please check individual event listings for pricing details and registration information.

Duration:

8 hours (4 hour per day)Event durations vary depending on the type and format of the event. Participants should plan to commit to the full duration of the event for maximum benefit and engagement.

Note:

  • Relevant links will be sent via email after registration.
  • Tickets are NON REFUNDABLE
  • Please remember to carry your Personal effects and bible
  • Accomodation is provided
