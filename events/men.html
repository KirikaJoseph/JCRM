Men Conference

Description:At JCRM, our Men's Conferences are dynamic gatherings designed to empower and equip men to live out their faith with courage, integrity, and purpose. Through engaging speakers, practical workshops, and impactful sessions, men are challenged to grow spiritually, lead their families with wisdom, and make a positive impact in their communities. These conferences provide a safe space for men to connect, share, and learn from one another as they navigate the challenges and opportunities of life. Join us as we gather to encounter God, strengthen brotherhood, and embark on a journey of growth and transformation.

Topics Covered:

  • Biblical Manhood
  • Leadership and Responsibility
  • Spiritual Growth
  • Fatherhood and Family
  • Personal Development
  • Community Engagement

Eligibility:

Open to all men seeking spiritual growth, leadership development, and fellowship. Attendees are encouraged to come with an open heart and a willingness to learn and grow together.

Lessons you will Learn

  • Discover biblical manhood and find identity in Christ.
  • Learn leadership skills for life's responsibilities.
  • Deepen relationship with God through spiritual practices.
  • Strengthen family bonds as fathers and husbands.
  • Grow personally in emotional, mental, and physical health.
  • Serve the community and make a positive impact.

Prerequisite:

None. All men are welcome to attend with an open heart.

Fee:

  • General Admission: $50
  • Early Bird Special (Limited Availability): $40
  • Group Discount (5 or more attendees): $45 per person
  • Student Rate (with valid student ID): $30
  • VIP Package (Includes premium seating and exclusive access): $100

Duration:

8 hours (4 hour per day)

Note:

  • Relevant links will be sent via email after registration.
  • Tickets are NON REFUNDABLE
  • Please remember to carry your Personal effects and bible
  • Accomodation is provided
