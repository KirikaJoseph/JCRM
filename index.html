Who we are

Jesus Come Revival Ministries (JCRM) is a vibrant community dedicated to spreading the transformative love of Jesus Christ. Rooted in faith and fueled by a passion for revival, we welcome all to join us on a journey of worship, discipleship, and service. Together, let's encounter Jesus and impact the world.

Welcome to Jesus Come Revival Ministries (JCRM)!

Experience the power of Jesus' love and join us on a journey of faith, revival, and transformation. Together, let's encounter God's presence and spread His life-changing message to the world. You're invited to be part of our vibrant community!

Church Spotlight

Explore our vibrant ministries, events, and community outreach efforts. Stay connected with our latest updates and inspiring stories.

Outreach and Missions

Our Outreach and Missions department is dedicated to extending the love of Christ beyond our walls. Through local outreach initiatives and global missions, we strive to meet the physical and spiritual needs of communities near and far.

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is a dynamic and engaging space where young people encounter God, build meaningful relationships, and grow in their faith. Through relevant teaching, fun activities, and mentorship, we empower youth to become passionate followers of Jesus.

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry provides a nurturing and safe environment for kids to learn about Jesus and develop a foundation of faith. Through interactive lessons, creative activities, and caring volunteers, we aim to inspire a lifelong love for God in our youngest members.

Bible Study Department

Our Bible Study Department offers opportunities for individuals to deepen their understanding of God's Word and grow in spiritual maturity. Through various study groups and resources, we encourage lifelong learning and application of biblical principles in daily life.

Prayer and Intercessory

Our Prayer and Intercessory team is committed to covering our church, community, and beyond in prayer. We believe in the power of prayer to bring healing, breakthrough, and transformation. Join us as we seek God's will and intercede for His purposes to be fulfilled.

Praise and Worship

Our Praise and Worship ministry is a vital part of our church's worship experience. With heartfelt praise and authentic worship, we exalt Jesus and invite His presence to dwell among us. Join us as we lift our voices and hearts in adoration of our Savior.
